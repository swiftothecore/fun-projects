the rules

qualifying by digit sum

A Friday qualifies when the individual digits of its day number plus its month number equal thirteen, or when the day number itself added to the month number equals thirteen.

Fri 8 May 2026
8+5=13
Fri 24 July 2026
2+4+7=13

Friday the 13th

Any Friday where the date itself falls on the thirteenth. Occasionally a Friday the 13th also qualifies by digit sum — when it falls in September, since 1 + 3 + 9 = 13.

qualifying with the year

A Friday also qualifies when the digit sum of the day, plus the month, plus the digit sum of the year all together equal thirteen.

Fri 2 Jan 2026
2+1+10=13
Fri 5 Mar 2021
5+3+5=13
